Why recycle cans and metals?







Metal is used for building new homes and railways.




kitchen utensils are made of stainless steel.

mercury is used for making thermometers.



aluminium is used for building planes , ships and space shuttles.



titanium is used for developing medical implants and strongly resists corrosion.



These are just some of the things your recycled waste can do. Please help recycle metals to help govan`s economy bloom and at the same time making a better world for all of us.
