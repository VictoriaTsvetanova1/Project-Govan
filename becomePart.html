GreenerGovan

Join us in helping save the planet



Become part of a community that seeks to improve both the town and the country. By recycling your waste, you help save many animals, plants and beautiful lands from being polluted. Govan can become an example of sustainability just by creating a habit of separating its waste into different categories, in order to help better recycle materials that can even be used for housing. With these methods it can be safe to say that Govan can be on track with eventually reaching Scotland`s goal of recycling waste of up to 70%.
